DeGoogle Guide

Take control of your digital life—one step at a time.

Why DeGoogle?

How Google Collects Data

  • Searches: Every query is logged and linked to your profile.
  • Location: Tracks your movements via Maps, Android, and even photos.
  • Emails & Docs: Scans content for keywords and context.
  • Browsing: Chrome, Analytics, and AdSense track your web activity.
  • Apps & Devices: Knows which apps you use, for how long, and on which devices.

Value & Cost of Your Data

Your data helps Google make billions from targeted ads. Each user is worth up to $200/year in ad revenue!

But the real cost: Personalized ads, higher prices, filter bubbles, and loss of privacy.

Why Should You Care?

  • Manipulation: Data is used to influence your choices and opinions.
  • Security: Data leaks and hacks can expose your private life.
  • Freedom: Profiling can affect opportunities, prices, and even travel.
  • Ethics: Support companies that respect your rights.
Did you know? Google services are free because your data is the real product. Every search, map, and email helps build a profile about you, which is used to target ads and influence what you see online.

For everyone: DeGoogling doesn't mean giving up convenience! You can still enjoy modern tech—just with more privacy and choice. Start with small steps, like changing your search engine or browser.

Alternatives to Google Products

Search: DuckDuckGo, Startpage, Brave Search
No tracking, private results.
Maps: Organic Maps, OsmAnd
Offline, open-source, no location history.
Email: ProtonMail, Tutanota
End-to-end encrypted, no ads.
Browser: Firefox, Brave, Vivaldi
Blocks trackers, open-source.
Cloud: Nextcloud, Proton Drive
Private file storage, your data stays yours.
Android: /e/OS, GrapheneOS
No Google Play, privacy by default.
Video: PeerTube, Vimeo
No ads, no tracking, community-driven.
Docs: CryptPad, OnlyOffice
Collaborate without surveillance.
Calendar: Proton Calendar, Etar
No data mining, open-source.
Photos: Immich, PhotoPrism
Private photo storage, AI on your device.
